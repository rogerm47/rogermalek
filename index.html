Understanding UK Roofing Costs

All costs are estimates and can vary based on location, materials, and complexity. Data based on 2025 market analysis.

Average Replacement Cost (Pitched Roof)

  • Terraced House: £5,000 - £8,000
  • Semi-Detached House: £6,000 - £10,000
  • Detached House: £8,000 - £15,000+

Cost Per Square Metre (m²) by Material

  • Concrete Tiles: £50 - £90 per m²
  • Natural Slate: £90 - £180 per m²
  • EPDM Rubber (Flat): £80 - £120 per m²
  • Felt (Flat): £50 - £80 per m²

Key Factors Affecting Price

Your final quote will depend on several variables:

  • Size and pitch of the roof
  • Choice of materials
  • Scaffolding hire costs
  • Waste removal and skip hire
  • Condition of underlying roof structure
  • Number of chimneys or skylights
  • Geographic location (e.g., London vs. North)
  • Ease of access to the property
